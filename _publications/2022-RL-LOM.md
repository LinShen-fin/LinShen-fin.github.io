This paper introduces an information-based reinforcement learning to exploit information channels to traders’ trading behavior in an equilibrium limit order market. Anticipating that informed traders are more likely to submit market buy (sell) orders when asset is significantly under (over) valued, uninformed traders tend to chase market buy (sell) orders of the informed to buy (sell). To gain from the order chasing of the uninformed, informed traders strategically submit more market buy (sell) and limit sell (buy) orders. This amplifies the order chasing behaviour of the uninformed, generating predictable trading behaviours that can improve information efficiency but reduce market liquidity.
